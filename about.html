About Us


Making A Difference

At Raneh Christian School Network, students receive so much more than an ordinary education. We provide ourselves on creating a unique, exciting environment that embraces all types of learners and promotes growth both academically and personally. Students and families are also welcomed into a dynamic and supportive community.

Our Mission



To provide quality, accelerated primary education to overage kids for the purpose of successful literacy.

To develop a morally disciplined and skillfully competent new generation of leaders.
